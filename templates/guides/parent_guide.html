Managing Your Children's Accounts

  • Monitor wishlist activities and get notifications
  • Review and approve wishlist items before they're public
  • Control who can see and interact with wishlists
  • Keep track of gift purchases to avoid duplicates

Safety Features

  • Private sharing links that only work for invited family and friends
  • Parent approval required for all wishlist changes
  • Secure account management with two-factor authentication
  • Granular privacy controls for each wishlist

Tips for Success

Prioritization

Help children organize their wishes by importance using our priority levels.

Details Matter

Encourage detailed item descriptions to help gift-givers make the right choice.

Early Planning

Share lists well before occasions to give family enough time to coordinate.

Stay Updated

Regularly review and update wishlists to keep them current and relevant.
