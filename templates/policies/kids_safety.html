Kids Safety Guide

Hey kids! Here are some important tips to stay safe while using Wishlistly!

Keep Personal Info Private

  • Never share your real name
  • Don't post your address
  • Keep your school name secret

Password Power

  • Use a strong password
  • Never share your password
  • Tell a parent if you forget it

Online Friends

  • Only share wishlists with people you know
  • Ask parents before sharing
  • Don't chat with strangers

Tell a Grown-Up If...

  • Someone asks for personal info
  • You see something scary
  • You feel uncomfortable

Remember!

If you ever feel unsure or scared about something online, always tell your parents or a trusted grown-up right away!
