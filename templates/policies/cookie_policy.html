What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. They help us make Wishlistly work better and provide a more personalized experience for you and your child.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

  • Required for basic website functionality
  • Enable secure login and authentication
  • Remember your session information
  • Cannot be disabled

Functional Cookies

  • Remember your preferences
  • Customize wishlist themes
  • Save sharing settings
  • Optional but recommended

Analytics Cookies

  • Help us understand how our service is used
  • Improve user experience
  • Completely anonymous
  • Can be disabled

How We Use Cookies

Security

  • Protect user accounts
  • Prevent unauthorized access
  • Maintain secure sessions

Personalization

  • Remember theme preferences
  • Customize user experience
  • Save language settings

Performance

  • Improve loading times
  • Monitor site performance
  • Enhance user experience

Managing Cookies

Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Here's how to manage cookies in popular browsers:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
Note: Disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality

Third-Party Cookies

We use a limited number of third-party services to enhance our website:

  • Google Analytics (anonymous usage data)
  • Content Delivery Networks (improved performance)
  • Security services (fraud prevention)
