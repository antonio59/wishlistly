Parent's Guide to Wishlistly

This guide helps you understand how Wishlistly works and how to keep your child safe while using it.

Account Management

Parent Account Features:

  • Monitor your child's wishlist activities
  • Approve sharing settings
  • Manage privacy preferences
  • Control who can view wishlists

Child Account Restrictions:

  • Limited to creating and editing wishlists
  • Cannot share wishlists without parent approval
  • No direct messaging capabilities
  • No personal information displayed publicly

Safety Features

Privacy Protection:

  • Private by default settings
  • No location tracking
  • No public profiles
  • Secure sharing links

Content Filtering:

  • Age-appropriate content only
  • No external links without approval
  • Automated inappropriate content detection
  • Manual content review process

Monitoring Tools

Activity Monitoring:

  • View wishlist creation history
  • Track sharing activities
  • Review item additions
  • Monitor account changes

Notifications:

  • New wishlist alerts
  • Sharing request notifications
  • Account activity updates
  • Security alerts

Best Practices

  1. Review the Kids Safety Guide with your child
  2. Set clear rules about sharing wishlists
  3. Regularly check account activity
  4. Keep login credentials secure
  5. Discuss online safety regularly
