For Parents

At Wishlistly, we take your child's privacy very seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your child's personal information.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Username and password
  • Parent's Email: Used for account verification and notifications
  • Wishlist Content: Items added to wishlists

How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain the Wishlistly service
  • To protect the security of accounts
  • To send important service updates

GDPR Compliance

Under GDPR, we provide the following rights:

  • Right to access your child's personal data
  • Right to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to delete your child's account
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability

Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your child's information:

  • Secure password storage using encryption
  • Regular security audits
  • Strict access controls

Parental Controls

Parents can:

  • Review their child's wishlist content
  • Modify privacy settings
  • Delete their child's account
  • Request data export
