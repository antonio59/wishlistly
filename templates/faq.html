Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Wishlistly is a safe and fun platform for kids to create and manage their wishlists while being supervised by their parents. It helps families organize gift-giving occasions and teaches children about prioritizing and managing their wishes.

Yes, Wishlistly is completely free to use! We believe in making wishlist management accessible to all families.

For Parents

As a parent, you have full visibility of your children's wishlists through your dashboard. You can view, approve, or suggest modifications to items they add.

After logging in to your parent account, click on the "Add Child" button in the navigation bar. Fill in your child's details and create their account. They'll receive their own login credentials.

For Kids

Once you're logged in, go to your dashboard and click "Create Wishlist" or "Add Item" to an existing wishlist. Fill in the details about what you want, and don't forget to add a picture if you can find one!

Safety & Privacy

Yes, we take privacy very seriously. All personal information is encrypted, and wishlists are only visible to approved family members and friends. Parents have full control over privacy settings.

By default, wishlists are private and only visible to parents. Parents can choose to share wishlists with specific family members or friends using secure sharing links.
