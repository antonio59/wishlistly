About Wishlistly

What is Wishlistly?

Wishlistly is a modern wishlist platform designed to make gift-giving more meaningful and organized. Our platform helps children create and share their wishlists while giving parents and loved ones an easy way to coordinate gifts.

Our Mission

We believe in making special occasions truly special. Our mission is to create a safe, fun, and easy-to-use platform where children can express their wishes and adults can fulfill them with confidence.

Key Features

  • Priority Levels: Organize wishes by importance (Must Have, Would Love, Nice to Have)
  • Beautiful Themes: Personalize wishlists with fun, seasonal themes
  • Safe Sharing: Share wishlists securely with family and friends
  • Gift Coordination: Prevent duplicate gifts with our purchase tracking

For Parents

Wishlistly provides tools to help parents:

  • Monitor and approve children's wishlists
  • Share wishlists with family and friends
  • Track purchased items
  • Receive notifications about wishlist activities

For Children

Children can:

  • Create beautiful wishlists with fun themes
  • Add items from their favorite stores
  • Organize wishes by importance
  • Share their lists with family
