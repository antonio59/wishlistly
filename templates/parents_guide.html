Parent's Guide to Wishlistly

Welcome to Wishlistly!

We're here to help you create and manage wishlists for your children in a safe and fun way. This guide will walk you through everything you need to know.

Getting Started

  1. Create an account to manage your children's wishlists
  2. Add your child's information and preferences
  3. Start creating wishlists for different occasions
  4. Share wishlists with family and friends

Safety & Privacy Features

  • Private sharing options - only people you choose can see your wishlists
  • No personal information is publicly displayed
  • Secure gift purchase tracking

Managing Wishlists

Creating a wishlist is easy:

  1. Click "Create New Wishlist"
  2. Choose an occasion (birthday, holiday, etc.)
  3. Add items from our catalog or custom items
  4. Set privacy preferences
  5. Share with family and friends

You can share wishlists in several ways:

  • Direct link sharing
  • Email invitations
  • Family group sharing

Keep track of gifts while maintaining surprises:

  • See which items have been purchased (without your child knowing)
  • Prevent duplicate gifts
  • Thank-you note tracking

Tips for Success

  • Keep wishlists updated throughout the year
  • Include items at various price points
  • Add notes or preferences for each item
  • Review lists with your child regularly

Need Help?

Our support team is here to help! Contact us at support@wishlistly.gift
